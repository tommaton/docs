## How long are Payment Links valid for?

Payment links are valid for up to 2 -5 days. But once any processing starts, for example end user enter their phone number or payment information, the respective link cannot be reused except for that transaction in transition

## Payment statuses

Within the MyXspend platform there are a number of payment statuses, these are as follows:

- **PENDING**: payment link generated but no payment attempted
- **INPROCESS**: End user started the payment process
- **FAILED**: payment attempted but failed
- **EXPIRED**: payment link expired (2-48 hours)
- **SUCCESSFUL**: payment completed successfully
- **REFUNDED**: payment refunded
- **UNDERPAID**: payment made by end user is less than original amount in the payment request
