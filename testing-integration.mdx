---
title: 'Testing MyXspend integration'
description: 'Test your MyXspend integration before going live with payments'
---

Before going live with MyXspend integration we recommend testing your integration with our platform to ensure that everything is working. Our staging platform will allow you to simulate payments without actually making live payments (we have test card payments to test).

It allows you to identitify any potentional issues or workflows before you go live.
